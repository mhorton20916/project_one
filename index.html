What is a Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrencies are cryptographically created digital tokens that can be used as a medium of exhange and a store of value without the need of physical tender.

What is the Blockchain?

Blockchain technology is a P2P network of individual computers that called nodes. Nodes validate the transactions using algorithims. Finished transaction are called blocks and are intertwined to the ledger history of coin.
